About Us

At HomeMade Pickles & Snacks, we bring to you the flavors of tradition packed with love and hygiene. From spicy pickles that remind you of grandma’s kitchen to crispy, fresh snacks for every mood — our homemade recipes are passed through generations and crafted with care. We use fresh ingredients, no preservatives, and ensure utmost cleanliness in our process. Whether you prefer tangy mango pickle or crunchy murukku, we’ve got your cravings covered! Your satisfaction and health are our priority. With both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options, our products cater to every taste bud.
